What is World Options Australia?

An Opportunity to Build
a Logistics Business

World Options Australia is an online shipping and logistics platform that helps Australian businesses compare couriers, manage freight, and reduce shipping costs. As a franchisee, you are the sales and relationship management arm of the business — we handle everything behind the scenes.

No trucks. No warehouses.

A relationship and technology-driven business. Your asset is your customer portfolio, not equipment or real estate.

Recurring monthly revenue

Customers ship every week or month. Your income compounds as your customer base grows over time.

Full operational support

Head Office manages carrier integrations, customer service, billing, and technology so you can focus on growth.

Work from anywhere

A laptop and a phone is all you need. Many franchise partners operate from a home office or on the road.
