Market Opportunity

Backed by One of Australia’s
Strongest Growth Industries

Australia’s Freight & Logistics market is forecast to grow from $103.2 billion in 2026 to $126.5 billion by 2031. Within that market, the Courier, Express & Parcel (CEP) segment — where World Options franchisees operate — is one of the fastest-growing categories, driven by structural shifts in how Australian businesses buy, sell and ship.

E-commerce

Continued growth in online retail keeps driving parcel and express delivery volumes.

SMEs

Nearly every Australian small and medium business ships — and most are still underserved on price and service.

Healthcare

Time-sensitive medical and pharmaceutical logistics continue to expand as an essential shipping category.

Omnichannel Retail

Retailers increasingly need flexible multi-carrier solutions to service online, in-store and click-and-collect orders.

International Trade

Growing import and export activity increases demand for reliable international courier and freight options.

Within this expanding market, the Courier, Express & Parcel (CEP) sector — the category World Options franchisees serve directly — is forecast to grow faster than the broader logistics industry, expanding annually as e-commerce, SME demand and international trade continue to accelerate.

The Revenue Model

How franchisees build income over time?

The World Options model is built on margin — the difference between the carrier buy rate negotiated by Head Office and the sell rate charged to your customers. As customers continue shipping, your income compounds month after month.

Win customers
Onboard Australian SMEs onto the platform and help them compare carrier pricing and services.
Customers keep shipping
Most customers ship every week or every month. Once onboarded, they tend to stay — creating a predictable income stream.
Revenue compounds
Each customer won today may still be generating monthly margin income years from now. The business becomes more valuable as it grows.

Specific income outcomes depend on the size and frequency of your customer base, local market conditions, and the effort applied to business development. World Options does not make guaranteed earnings claims. Existing franchise partners can share their experience during the Discovery Process.

The Technology

One platform. Every major carrier.

Shipping Portal

Your customers get access to a dedicated online shipping portal — a powerful, easy-to-use platform where they can quote, book, and manage all their freight in one place. It is your key tool for winning and retaining business.

Multi-carrier comparison

Customers compare live pricing, transit times, and service levels across FedEx, UPS, TNT, Aramex, and more — all in one place.

Domestic & international

Parcel delivery, pallet freight, express, economy, and international courier services all accessible through a single login.

Insurance & protection

Customers can add freight insurance and protection plans directly through the platform, adding value at every shipment.

Full shipment visibility

Real-time tracking and shipment management so your customers always know where their freight is and what it costs.

Customer service support

When customers have freight issues or carrier escalations, the Head Office support team steps in — so you stay focused on growth.

Automated billing

Head Office handles all invoicing and payment processing. Franchisees receive their margin without managing financial administration.

Is This Right for You?

The profile of a successful
World Options franchisee

There is no single background that predicts success — but the franchisees who grow fastest tend to share a common set of skills and motivations.

  • Strong B2B sales experience and relationship-building ability
  • Business development mindset with a focus on long-term accounts
  • Logistics, transport, or freight industry exposure (preferred, not essential)
  • Self-motivated with the drive to work independently
  • Desire to build a scalable business with recurring income
  • Clear commercial acumen and a professional communication style
  • Looking for greater control, flexibility, and income potential

If you can sell, you can win. This model directly rewards your ability to open doors and convert conversations into customers. The harder you work and the more relationships you build, the more your income grows. There is no cap on what you can earn.
